Why Choose Us?

the best coffee maker in the town

Many people don't realize that in order to get the right taste and flavor out of coffee it needs to be fresh.  Like, really fresh!  Imagine baking a fresh loaf of bread and then eating it 2 weeks later.  It would not be a good experience.  It would be very stale and the thing we most enjoy about fresh bread would be lost.  If all you have ever known is stale bread, it may not be a big deal.  But let's say, the next time you baked bread you ate it fresh from the oven?  The experience and flavor would be amazing.

our menu

Black Coffee

No frills here: Black coffee is made from plain ground coffee beans that are brewed hot. It's served without added sugar, milk, or flavorings.

₹.499

Cappuccino

This espresso-based drink is similar to a latte, but the frothy top layer is thicker. The standard ratio is equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam. It's often served in a 6-ounce cup and can be topped with a sprinkling of cinnamon.

₹.399

Mocha Latte

It is one of the sweetest kinds among all different types of coffee. A Mocha consists in a shot of espresso mixed with a spoon of chocolate powder.

₹.559

Irish Coffee

It is more commonly found on restaurants than in cafés because an Irish Coffee contains alcohol. This coffee is brewed with sugar, whiskey and a 2-3 cm layer of cream on top.

₹.599

Iced Coffee

Is there anything better than a glass of iced coffee on a hot day (or any day, for that matter)? The simplest way to make it: Brew a regular cup of hot coffee, then cool it over ice. Add whatever milk and sweeteners you like.

₹.449

Cold Brew

Cold brew is one of the biggest coffee trends of the last decade, and for good reason: It's made by slowly steeping coffee grounds over cool or room-temperature water, so it tastes smoother and less bitter than regular iced coffee, which is brewed hot.

₹.799
